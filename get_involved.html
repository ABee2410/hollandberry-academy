Clubs

Art club

In our art club we provide the best materials for our girls to create amazing masterpieces. We encourage them to be curious with their imagination and share and create new things and ideas with eachother .

Reading Program

Our reading program is for the students who love to read. In our library we have massive amounts of books in everyone's interest. Fiction, Mystery, Non-ficton, Horror, Romance, Comedy, Teen Relates, etc. We provide a quiet area for the girls to read and write about the theme of their books.

Science club

Our science club consists of many experiments and fun activites. There's lots of solving, caculating, and measuring. We always work in groups and work hard together. We make sure the girls are wearing all the saftey materials to perform the tasks assigned.

Sports

Tennis

Tennis is one of the best sports for exercise. We train our girls to be strong and play hard for the gold medal. We provide the most updated equipment and uniforms for them to have a wonderful and successful game.

Horse-back Riding

Here at Hollandberry Academy, we have practiced horse-back riding since the opening of the school. Many of our girls have earned and achieved many awards and goals. We push them to communicate with our trained horses and connect with them and practice.

Field Hockey

Our field hockey players generally have superior endurance, hand-eye coordination, agility, and overall strength. We make sure that they have the materials they need to play safely while enjoying it.
